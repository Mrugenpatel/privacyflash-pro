1. Personal Information Collection and Use

If you grant Workout with Friends permission, we may collect and use personal information from you as follows.

  • Location Services
    Purpose: We may request your location to show you nearby gyms.

  • Camera
    Purpose: This lets you take and upload photos.

  • Photos
    Purpose: This lets you save and upload photos.

Please note that you can decline a permission or revoke it after you granted it in the iOS settings. If you do so, we will not collect your personal information related to that permission. However, the functionality of Workout with Friends may be limited as a result.

We may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system.


2. Personal Information Sharing

Workout with Friends integrates various third party services. If you grant Workout with Friends permission, we may share your personal information as follows:

  • Location Services
    • InMobi
      Purpose: We use your location to show you ads for businesses nearby.

Workout with Friends integrates the following third party services that are not requesting any permission:

  • A-Star
  • SwiftKeychainWrapper
  • GCDWebServer
  • SwiftyJSON
  • SnapKit
  • swift-protobuf
  • MappaMundi
  • EarlGrey
  • onepassword-app-extension
  • Leanplum-iOS-SDK

All third party services may receive technical information about your device, for example, its IP address or operating system version. Also, they may keep your personal information over time and across different apps and websites.


3. Tracking Technologies

Workout with Friends and the third party services it integrates are using tracking technologies as follows:

  • Device Identifier
    • Workout with Friends (This App)
      Purpose: We use your device identifier to keep track of which devices yo are using.

These identifiers do not identify you by name. However, they identify your device and can be used to track you for purposes of personalized advertising. You can opt out from such tracking in the settings on your iOS device.

Workout with Friends is a mobile app and as such will not be responsive to Do-Not-Track (DNT) signals that you may have set up in your browser.


4. Social Logins

You can sign into Workout with Friends via Facebook, in which case we will receive personal information from your Facebook account. You can review and set which personal information we will receive at the time of the login. Also, Facebook will receive various information from us about you (e.g., that you are a user of Workout with Friends). The privacy policy of Facebook contains more information on this sharing of personal information. View Facebook's policy here.


5. In-app Purchase Information

Workout with Friends does not process any in-app purchase information.
